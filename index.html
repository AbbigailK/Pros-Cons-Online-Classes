The Pros and Cons of Online Classes

By: Abbigail Klein

The poll was unanimous and answered by students at the University of Maryland, College Park

Online or in-person classes?

(Image from google forms)

What is a pro to online classes?

  • 3% - Ability to sleep-in
  • 9% - Going at your own pace
  • 12% - Easier exams
  • 15% - Recorded lectures / asynchronous classes
    • A student at UMD stated, "The Recorded lectures- the best thing to happen from online classes. If you lose focus during class or need to rewatch a certain lecture it is super easy and clear (in terms of audio/visual). Lectures that were recorded in person in no way compared to zoom recorded lectures because it was hard to hear and see."
  • 33% - Not having to walk to class
  • 36% - Flexibility

What is a con to online classes?

  • 3% - Techinical difficulties
  • 3% - Everyting
  • 3% - Group projects
    • A student at UMD stated, "Group projects-members can be in different time zones and/or impossible to reach + hold accountability to."
  • 6% - Overload of work
  • 6% - Being stuck inside all day
  • 15% - Inability to meet students and teachers
  • 15% - Less engagement
  • 24% - Difficulty concerntrating
  • 33% - Lack of strucure, inability to learn
